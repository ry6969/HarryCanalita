SDG 12
Responsible Consumption and Production
Industrial symbiosis, a concept where industries collaborate to share resources, energy, and by-products, promotes efficient resource utilization and minimizes waste. By adopting this approach, industries can reduce their environmental footprint while fostering economic growth, aligning with the goals of SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production. Through strategic partnerships and innovation, industrial symbiosis transforms waste into valuable inputs, creating a circular economy that benefits both society and the environment.
